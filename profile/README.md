## 🧭 Who We Are
 
BinX Tech is a technology and innovation company that bridges the gap between academic learning and real industry needs. We provide the tools, mentorship, and ecosystem that transform potential into impact — empowering the next generation of innovators across Palestine and beyond.
 
> **Our Vision:** To become a leading technology and innovation ecosystem where students evolve into creators, innovators, and entrepreneurs — driving sustainable technological advancement across the region.
 
> **Our Mission:** To transform students from passive learners into active builders through practical learning, expert mentorship, community engagement, and real-world execution — preparing them for the global technology market.
