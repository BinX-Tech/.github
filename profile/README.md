## 🚀 Who We Are

**BinX Tech** is a Palestine-based technology startup founded in 2025, built around a single mission: transforming students from passive learners into active builders.

We operate an integrated innovation ecosystem that combines **software development services**, **technical education**, and a **startup incubator** — all under one roof. From web and mobile applications to AI solutions and cybersecurity, we deliver real-world technology while developing the next generation of builders.
